# Industry Adoption Stories and Lessons

Use these resources to understand how AI adoption succeeds or fails inside real organizations. This category is intentionally selective: the goal is to learn deployment judgment, not collect every customer story.

## Success Stories With Deployment Detail

- [Klarna AI assistant does the work of 700 full-time agents](https://openai.com/index/klarna/) - Useful for studying measurable customer-service automation: volume, resolution time, repeat inquiries, customer satisfaction, and operating model implications.
- [Morgan Stanley uses AI evals to shape the future of financial services](https://openai.com/index/morgan-stanley/) - Useful because it emphasizes evals, expert review, and controlled deployment in a regulated knowledge workflow.
- [Morgan Stanley Research Announces AskResearchGPT](https://www.morganstanley.com/press-releases/morgan-stanley-research-announces-askresearchgpt) - First-party enterprise knowledge assistant story from the customer side, not only the vendor side.

## Failure and Risk Lessons

- [Air Canada chatbot liability case summary](https://www.americanbar.org/groups/business_law/resources/business-law-today/2024-february/bc-tribunal-confirms-companies-remain-liable-information-provided-ai-chatbot/) - Useful reminder that customer-facing AI needs ownership, accuracy controls, and accountability; companies remain responsible for AI-provided information.
- [AI Incident Database: Air Canada chatbot report](https://incidentdatabase.ai/reports/3673/) - Incident-oriented record for studying AI failure modes and governance implications.
- [RAND: The Root Causes of Failure for Artificial Intelligence Projects and How They Can Succeed](https://www.rand.org/pubs/research_reports/RRA2680-1.html) - Broad failure analysis across AI projects; especially relevant for FDEs because miscommunication, weak problem framing, and data readiness often matter more than model choice.

## Case Study Libraries

- [OpenAI customer stories](https://openai.com/customer-stories/) - Vendor case library; use selectively and look for concrete workflow, metric, and rollout details.
- [Google Cloud customer stories](https://cloud.google.com/customers) - Broad cloud and AI customer story library; useful for finding industry patterns.
- [Microsoft AI customer stories](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-cloud/blog/2025/07/24/ai-powered-success-with-1000-stories-of-customer-transformation-and-innovation/) - Large case library; useful for scanning enterprise AI adoption patterns across functions.
