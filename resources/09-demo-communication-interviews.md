# Demo, Communication, and Interview Practice

Use these resources to turn technical work into a clear deployment story.

## Demo and Customer Storytelling

- [OpenAI customer stories](https://openai.com/customer-stories/) - Examples of AI value narratives.
- [Google Cloud customer stories](https://cloud.google.com/customers) - Customer outcome framing.
- [Product demo best practices search](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=technical+product+demo+best+practices) - Delivery examples for technical demos.

## Interview Practice Themes

- Use-case selection and rejection.
- Architecture tradeoffs.
- RAG vs tools vs agents vs deterministic workflow.
- Evals, reliability, and safety.
- Security and governance.
- Rollout, adoption, and business ownership.
- Ambiguity, conflict, and execution under pressure.
