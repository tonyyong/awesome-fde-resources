## Interview Practice Themes

- Use-case selection and rejection.
- Architecture tradeoffs.
- RAG vs tools vs agents vs deterministic workflow.
- Evals, reliability, and safety.
- Security and governance.
- Rollout, adoption, and business ownership.
- Ambiguity, conflict, and execution under pressure.
