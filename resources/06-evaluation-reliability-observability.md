# Evaluation, Reliability, and Observability

Use these resources to prove quality with evidence instead of demos and vibes.

## Evaluation

- [OpenAI Evals guide](https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/evals) - Evaluation design for model behavior.
- [Claude evaluation tool](https://platform.claude.com/docs/en/test-and-evaluate/eval-tool) - Claude-focused eval workflow.
- [Azure RAG evaluators](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/foundry/concepts/evaluation-evaluators/rag-evaluators) - RAG-specific evaluation concepts.

## Reliability and Observability

- [OpenTelemetry docs](https://opentelemetry.io/docs/) - Standard observability concepts and instrumentation.
- [Google SRE book: monitoring distributed systems](https://sre.google/sre-book/monitoring-distributed-systems/) - Monitoring and alerting principles.
- [AWS AgentOps with Bedrock AgentCore](https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/machine-learning/agentops-operationalize-agentic-ai-at-scale-with-amazon-bedrock-agentcore/) - AgentOps concepts: traces, tasks, tools, and operations.
